Aims

The Oxford Dhoti Society promotes structured engagement with philosophical traditions and civilisational thoughts. Indigenous clothing—particularly the dhoti and analogous draped garments across civilisations—is examined as an instrument of southernisation, epistemic autonomy, and cultural continuity. The Society also studies ritual practices as embodiments of philosophical commitments.
